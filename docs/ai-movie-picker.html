🎬 A.I. Movie Picker

“Love, Logic & Late Night Cinema”

Deep beneath the layers of your streaming services... something lurks. An intelligence. A taste. A *vibe*.

Welcome to your new favorite weapon in the eternal battle of “What should we watch tonight?” – the A.I. Movie Picker. Whether you're watching solo, with your clone, or your apocalypse survival partner, this tool studies your preferences, your moods, and your previous picks to generate an eerily perfect movie suggestion.

It cross-references genres, tone, pacing, themes, visual style, and emotional resonance using a custom-trained model on thousands of personal reviews, Reddit threads, and known film nerd behavior. Then? It suggests one. Just one. And it's usually dead-on.
